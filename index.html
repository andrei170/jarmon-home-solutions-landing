EV Charger FAQ

Cost, permits, and panel questions, answered straight

How much does EV charger installation cost in Cincinnati?

It depends on your panel capacity, how far the charger sits from your panel, and whether a panel or service upgrade is needed. A straightforward Level 2 install near an adequate panel costs less than one that needs a new circuit run across the home or a panel upgrade. You get a clear written quote before any work starts, so there are no surprises.

Do I need a permit to install an EV charger in Ohio?

Yes. A hardwired Level 2 charger is a permanent electrical circuit, so it needs an electrical permit and inspection in most Ohio jurisdictions. As a licensed electrician registered with the City of Cincinnati and the State of Ohio, we pull the permit and handle the inspection as part of the job.

Can my electrical panel handle an EV charger?

Often yes, but a Level 2 charger draws significant continuous load, so it comes down to your panel's size and how much is already in use. We check your panel before install. If capacity is tight, we recommend a panel or service upgrade and quote it up front.

How long does installation take?

A typical Level 2 install is done in a single visit once the charger and parts are on hand. Jobs that need a panel upgrade or a long circuit run take longer. We confirm the timeline when we quote the work.

Do you install Tesla and non-Tesla chargers?

Yes. We install Tesla wall connectors and universal J1772 chargers that work with most other EV brands, and we help you choose the right charger for your vehicle and your home's setup.

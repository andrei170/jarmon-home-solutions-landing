EV & Electrical FAQ

How much does an EV charger install cost in Cincinnati?+
It depends on the distance from your panel, whether a panel upgrade is needed, and the charger model. We assess quickly and give a clear, upfront quote before any work starts.
Do I need a panel upgrade for an EV charger?+
Sometimes. A Level 2 charger draws real power, so older or near-capacity panels may need an upgrade. We check first and tell you straight.
